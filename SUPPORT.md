## Before Opening an Issue

1. **Search existing issues** — your problem may already be tracked.
2. **Check the [CHANGELOG](CHANGELOG.md)** — the behaviour may have changed
   intentionally in a recent release.
3. **Read the [spec](.openspec/specs/)** — the acceptance criteria describe
   what the feature is *supposed* to do.
4. **Reproduce on the latest release** — bug reports against older versions
   get lower priority unless they're security issues.

## Response Times

This project is maintained on a best-effort basis. Typical response windows:

- **Security reports** — see [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) (2 business days to
  acknowledge)
- **Bug reports** — within 1 week
- **Feature requests** — triaged in monthly review; may be closed with
  "won't implement" if out of scope
- **Questions** — best-effort; Discussions are community-answered
